CODE-ATHON
What is Code-Athon?
It is for students and beginners with any background to participate and hone their coding skills, by competing against other students of their level.

Code-Athon has 3 levels :
Beginners || Intermediate || Pro

Compete with coders of the same level and win Exciting rewards.
Our Motivation
What Should You Expect?
Exposure
Exposure to competitive coding, implementation of your theoretical knowledge.
Brainstorming
Brainstorm, create, test! You will have 180 minutes to submit your code.
Innovations
Create an innovative idea! We believe the new generation can open the best ways in problem solving.
Honors
Winner will get CASH prize and certificate of appreciation.
Tracks of the Hackathon
Game innovations with Project Morpheus
Project Morpheus offers an opportunity to work on PlayStation and virtual space, so come together to work with new sceneries, designs, and code.
Social services
New ideas for banking, time management, social living, meeting, sharing, and others. Improve social life and communications with your ideas!
VR Advertising
Advertising is everywhere. Catch a chance to become a part of its reinvention. Create new conceptions for VR marketing in real life!
Learning and education
Research has been done on learning in virtual reality, as its immersive qualities may enhance learning. Maybe you are the one who knows how to learn faster and more efficiently!
Product development
This track is about innovations in real-life products. Any product can always be improved. Or maybe you will design something special, something we didn’t even think about?
Evaluation Criteria
On the basis of the following criteria, your code will be evaluated and ranked.
Runtime Complexity
Total time taken for the execution of code and pass all the test .
Space Complexity
Total memory space used by the code while its execution.
Solution Size
size of your code.
